## Ingredients

* 170 g milk
* 130 g water
* 400	g wheat flour
* 100 g spelt flour
* 35 g butter
* 15 g fresh yeast
* 10 g salz
* 15 g honey

## Preparation

* Mix for 5-10 minutes.

* Prove for 30 minutes.

* Form a loaf and prove for another 15 minutes.

* Bake for 35 minutes at 200 Â°C.
